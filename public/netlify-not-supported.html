EspoCRM is not supported on Netlify

EspoCRM requires a PHP runtime together with a supported database. Netlify deployments are limited to static assets and serverless functions, so the application cannot run from this preview.

To evaluate EspoCRM, follow one of the installation options outlined in our documentation. The Netlify guide explains why this platform is incompatible and lists the recommended hosting targets.

You can safely close this preview and continue with an environment that offers PHP 8.2 or newer and a supported database such as MySQL, MariaDB, or PostgreSQL.
